Privacy Policy

Cyberia Coffee stores only the information required for the operation of the board. This includes the text of posts and any files uploaded by users. No account registration is required to participate.

Board Rules

  • No pornography.
  • No graphic gore.
  • Spam and automated posting are prohibited.
  • Distribution of copyrighted or non-free software is prohibited.
  • Respect other users and avoid disruptive behavior.
  • Moderators may remove content that harms the board.
